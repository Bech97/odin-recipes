Butter Chicken

Description

A delicious, simple winter meal with plenty spice and fat

Ingredients

  • 60g Tomato Purée
  • 105g Greek Yoghurt
  • 10g Garam Masala
  • 5g Chili Powder
  • 4 cloves of Garlic
  • 8g Ginger
  • 45ml Mango Chutney
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1000g Chopped Chicken Breast
  • 150ml Water
  • 45ml Single Cream
  • Handful coriander to top off

Procedure

  1. Blend yoghurt, spices and tomato purée
  2. Pour it over the chicken, leave it for 30+ minutes in the fridge to let marinate.
  3. Heat and cook chicken on a high temperature
  4. When chicken is tender and thoroughly cooked, add cream, water to balance the sauce
  5. Top off the dish with coriander and mango chutney, supplement with rice
